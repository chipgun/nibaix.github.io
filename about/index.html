About

I’m an ICer and has been working in semiconductor industry for over 20 years, particularly professional in design areas of wireless communication, low-power microcontroller, and RISC processor.

Summary

  • Have been dedicated to semiconductor industry for 20 years, and engaged in engineering management over 10 years.
  • Be capable of building competitive team and inspiring motivation, collaboration and productivity.
  • Seamless team work across multiple functional organizations making productive delivery.
  • Solid technical skills and full flow in state-of-the-art IP/SoC design, verification, DFT, timing closure, power signoff, and tapeout.
  • Profoundly understand and master mixed-signal and low-power technology applied in IoT products.
  • Market-proven experience in wireless technology with several mass productions, of which 100+ million chip units have been rolled out.
  • Excellent track record in organizing or hands-on 50+ times chip tapeout in variety of process nodes ranging from 180nm to 22nm.

Current Mission

I’m now engaging in running a design house Chipgun Semiconductor Design, providing ASIC/SoC/FPGA design services. We supply customers with comprehensive IC flow, aka Spec-2-GDSII. Cutting edge technologies and methodologies are employed for adding values for customers’ products and enabling customers much more successful.
